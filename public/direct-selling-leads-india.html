Direct Selling Lead Challenges India Mein

Yeh 6 challenges har direct seller face karta hai — LeadFlow sab solve karta hai.

🚩 Consistent Lead Source Nahi

Referrals kabhi milte hain kabhi nahi. Warm market khatam. Facebook Lead Ads se consistent daily leads milti hain — business ka scale predictable ho jaata hai.

🚩 High Cost Per Lead

Galat targeting se paise waste hote hain. Product-specific audience target karo — health products ke liye health-conscious, beauty ke liye relevant demographics. CPL ₹40-150 possible hai.

🚩 Cold Calling Rejection

Strangers ko cold call karna — 95% rejection frustrating hai. Facebook Lead Ads se leads khud form fill karti hain — inbound inquiry hai, cold call nahi. Conversion naturally better hoti hai.

🚩 Koi Team Distribution Nahi

Ek leader saari leads leke baithta hai, baaki team members bhooke rehte hain. LeadFlow ka round-robin system fair distribution ensure karta hai — sabko equal chance milta hai daily.

🚩 Excel Mein Manual Tracking

Excel sheets outdated hain — data entry error, lost leads, no real-time visibility. LeadFlow dashboard mein sab kuch real-time track hota hai — koi lead kabhi miss nahi hoti.

🚩 Koi Mobile System Nahi

Desktop pe CRM manage karna field sales ke liye impractical hai. LeadFlow fully mobile-optimized hai — team members phone pe hi sab manage karte hain, on the go.
