Ek Modicare member 15 leads le jaata hai, doosre ko 0 milta. Team mein frustration. Daily limit — 5/day ya 10/day — se sabko fair chance milta hai roz automatically.

🚩 Night Leads Waste

Raat ko Vestige lead aati hai, koi online nahi. Subah tak cold ho jaati hai. LeadFlow Night Backlog feature se raat ki leads safe hoti hain aur subah 10 AM pe automatically distribute hoti hain.
