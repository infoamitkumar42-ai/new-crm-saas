MLM Lead Generation Mein Kya Problems Aati Hain?

Yeh 6 problems almost har MLM person face karta hai — aur LeadFlow in sab ka solution hai.

🚩 Lead Source Ki Clarity Nahi

Pata nahi leads kahan se aayengi. Friends list khatam, cold calling frustrating, aur koi system nahi. Facebook Lead Ads ek proven aur scalable source hai — daily fresh leads guaranteed.

🚩 Manual Lead Tracking Fail Hota Hai

Excel sheets, WhatsApp groups, paper notes — sab jugaad hai, system nahi. Leads duplicate hoti hain, miss hoti hain, follow-up bhool jaate hain. LeadFlow ek centralized dashboard deta hai.

🚩 Friends/Family List Khatam

Har MLM person yahi problem face karta hai — warm market khatam. Facebook Lead Ads se roz naye strangers lead form fill karte hain jo genuinely interested hain. Warm market ki zaroorat nahi.

🚩 Facebook Ads Bina Automation Ke

Ad chalate hain, leads aati hain, lekin manually manage karte karte din nikal jaata hai. Koi automation nahi to koi ek hi lead pe teen baar call karta hai, koi lead miss ho jaati hai.

🚩 Team Distribution System Nahi

Team ke 5-10 members hain lekin leads manually divide karte ho. Kisi ko zyada milti hain, kisi ko kam. LeadFlow ka round-robin system sabko fair aur equal leads deta hai automatically.

🚩 Daily Follow-Up Chaos

Kal ki leads aaj follow up karo, aaj ki kal — koi structure nahi. LeadFlow mein har lead ka status track hota hai, push notification milti hai, aur koi lead kabhi bhool nahi sakti.
