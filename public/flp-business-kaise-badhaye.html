FLP Business Growth Mein Kya Rok Raha Hai?

Yeh 6 specific FLP challenges LeadFlow solve karta hai.

🚩 Koi Lead System Nahi

Roz kisi na kisi se puchho, referral maango — yeh sustainable nahi hai. Facebook Lead Ads se ek system set karo jo roz automatically fresh FLP leads deliver kare bina extra effort ke.

🚩 Manual WhatsApp Chaos

Lead aai, WhatsApp pe share ki, 3 log ek hi pe call karte hain. Prospect ka experience kharab hota hai aur deal loose hoti hai. LeadFlow mein ek lead = ek member = zero confusion.

🚩 Team Inactive Hai

Members join karte hain lekin kaam nahi karte kyunki unhe fresh leads nahi milti. Jab roz automatically leads milti hain to team motivated aur active rehti hai. Engagement naturally improve hoti hai.

🚩 Daily Limit Nahi

Koi member 15 leads le jaata hai, koi 2. Unfair distribution se team mein tension hoti hai. LeadFlow mein daily limit set karo — sab ko equal aur fair chance milta hai har roz.

🚩 Night Leads Waste Hoti Hain

Raat 11 baje FLP lead aati hai, koi online nahi hota, morning tak cold ho jaati hai. LeadFlow "Night Backlog" mein safe karta hai aur subah 10 AM pe automatically distribute kar deta hai.

🚩 Progress Visibility Nahi

Manager ko pata nahi kaun kitna active hai, kitni leads assign huin, kaunsi pending hain. LeadFlow manager dashboard mein sab kuch real-time visible hai — koi blind spot nahi.
