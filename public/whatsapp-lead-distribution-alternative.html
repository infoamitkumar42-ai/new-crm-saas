WhatsApp Lead Sharing Ki Yeh Problems Hain

Agar aap bhi WhatsApp pe leads share karte hain, toh yeh problems zaroor face ki hogi.

🚩 Screenshot Sharing Mein Delay

Lead aati hai, manager screenshot leta hai, group mein bhejta hai. Is sab mein 30 minute se 2 ghante lag jaate hain. Tab tak prospect thanda ho jaata hai. LeadFlow mein lead aate hi 10 seconds mein assign.

🚩 Multiple Members Same Lead Ko Call Karte Hain

WhatsApp group mein lead dikhti hai — 2-3 members ek saath call karte hain. Prospect irritate hota hai, brand image kharab hoti hai. LeadFlow mein ek lead sirf ek member ko — koi overlap nahi.

🚩 Messages Group Flood Mein Kho Jaate Hain

WhatsApp group mein aur bhi messages aate hain — lead wali message neeche dab jaati hai. Koi member scroll karke dhundhta nahi. LeadFlow mein lead directly assigned member ke dashboard pe — koi miss nahi.

🚩 Koi Tracking Nahi — Andhera

WhatsApp mein koi track nahi hota — kisne call kiya, kisne nahi kiya, lead warm hai ya thandi. Manager ko manually poochna padta hai. LeadFlow dashboard mein sab real-time — koi dark spot nahi.

🚩 No Daily Limits — Koi Fairness Nahi

WhatsApp group mein jo pehle respond kare, lead uski. Senior members ya fast typists zyada leads le jaate hain, juniors ko kuch nahi milta. LeadFlow ka round-robin system 100% fair hai.

🚩 Manager Queries Se Flood Ho Jaata Hai

"Manager bhai, yeh lead mujhe mili hai ya nahi?" — din mein 20 baar yeh message aata hai. Koi structured system nahi toh manager ki poori energy answering queries mein. LeadFlow se sab clear — koi query nahi.
