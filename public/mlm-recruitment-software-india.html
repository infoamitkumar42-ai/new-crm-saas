MLM Recruitment Mein Kya Challenges Hain?

Yeh 6 recruitment problems LeadFlow solve karta hai.

🚩 Recruitment Leads Nahi Hain

Warm market khatam, cold calling frustrating. Facebook Lead Ads se roz naye interested prospects recruit hone ke liye ready milte hain — bina ek bhi cold call ke.

🚩 Manual Outreach Exhausting Hai

Roz manually 20-30 log dhundhna, pitch dena, rejection face karna — mentally draining hai. Automated lead generation aur distribution se focus pitch pe hota hai, dhundhne pe nahi.

🚩 Friends List Khatam

Sabne FLP join kar liya, ab koi naya nahi hai. Facebook Lead Ads se strangers ki unlimited supply milti hai — India mein 400 million+ Facebook users hain, aapka audience nahi khatam hoga.

🚩 Koi Tracking Nahi — Kaun Kaam Kar Raha Hai

Manager ko pata nahi kaun recruitment call kar raha hai, kaun nahi. LeadFlow dashboard mein sab visible hai — kaun active hai, kitni leads assign huin, kaun follow-up nahi kar raha.

🚩 Duplicate Approaches

Do members ek hi prospect ko approach karte hain — prospect irritate, deal loose. LeadFlow mein round-robin system ensure karta hai ki ek lead exactly ek member ke paas jaati hai.

🚩 Koi Daily Structure Nahi

Kabhi 0 leads, kabhi 20 — koi consistency nahi. Daily limit set karo — 5/day ya 10/day. Roz consistent number se team members disciplined aur structured rehte hain.
