FLP WhatsApp groups mein lead screenshots share karna ek wasteful process hai. Koi track nahi, koi accountability nahi. LeadFlow directly assign karta hai — koi forward-sharing nahi.

🚩 Response Delay Se Recruitment Miss

FLP prospect ka interest window sirf 1-2 ghante ka hota hai. Late call karo toh woh kisi aur ke downline mein chala jaata hai. LeadFlow seconds mein assign karta hai taaki fresh prospect se baat ho.
