🚩 Slow Response = Lost Sale

Herbalife lead form fill karta hai, phir 5-6 ghante baad koi call karta hai. Tab tak us prospect ne dusre distributor se join kar liya hota hai. LeadFlow 10 seconds mein assign karta hai.

🚩 No Daily Limit Control
