🚩 WhatsApp Lead Chaos

Manager lead screenshot share karta hai group mein. 3 log ek hi lead pe call karte hain, 2 lead miss ho jaati hai. Yeh sab band hota hai LeadFlow se.

🚩 Slow Response Time

Facebook lead aati hai, 4 ghante baad koi call karta hai. Tab tak prospect dusre distributor se join kar chuka hota hai. LeadFlow seconds mein assign karta hai.

🚩 Unfair Distribution

Kuch team members zyada leads le jaate hain, kuch ko milti hi nahi. Round-robin system se sabko equal aur fair chance milta hai.

🚩 No Accountability

Manager ko pata nahi kaunsi lead kiske paas hai, kaun follow-up kar raha hai. Dashboard mein sab kuch real-time dikhta hai.

🚩 Night Leads Waste

Raat 10 baje lead aati hai — koi online nahi hota. LeadFlow unhe Night Backlog mein rakh ke subah 10 baje automatically distribute karta hai.

🚩 Daily Limit Nahi

Ek member 20 leads le jaata hai, doosre ko 0 milti. Daily limit set karo — starter pe 5/day, supervisor pe 10/day — automation sambhalti hai baaki.

FAQ — Forever Living Specific

FLP distributors ke common questions ke jawab.

FLP leads kaise distribute kare automatically?
LeadFlow CRM ko apni Forever Living Facebook Lead Ad se connect karo. Jab bhi koi lead form fill karta hai, system automatically sabse suitable team member ko assign kar deta hai — daily limit, online status aur plan ke hisaab se. Koi manual kaam nahi. Setup 10-15 minute mein complete ho jaata hai.
Forever Living team ke liye best CRM kaunsa hai?
LeadFlow CRM specifically Indian network marketing teams ke liye bana hai. Forever Living (FLP), insurance aur real estate teams use karti hain. Features mein hai: automatic round-robin assignment, daily lead limits, push notifications, aur manager dashboard — sabkuch FLP workflow ke saath perfectly fit hota hai. Koi generic CRM itna specific nahi hai.
Facebook leads Forever Living business mein kaise use kare?
Meta Ads Manager mein Facebook Lead Ad banao apne FLP products ke liye, LeadFlow CRM ka webhook URL connect karo. Jab lead aaye, woh automatically tumhare team member ke dashboard pe appear hogi push notification ke saath. Response time 4-6 ghante se ghatke seconds ho jaata hai — conversion rate significantly improve hoti hai.
LeadFlow CRM FLP ke saath kaise connect hota hai?
LeadFlow CRM Meta (Facebook) webhook ke through connect hota hai. Setup simple hai: admin panel mein team banao, members add karo, aur Facebook Lead Ad ka webhook URL LeadFlow pe point karo. Baaki sab automatic — koi coding nahi chahiye. Ek baar setup ke baad system khud chalata hai.
Kya raat ko aane wali FLP leads bhi assign hoti hain?
Haan. Raat 10 PM se subah 8 AM ke beech aane wali leads "Night Backlog" mein safe hoti hain aur subah 10 AM pe automatically active team members ko distribute ho jaati hain. Koi lead waste nahi hoti.
Ek se zyada FLP team manage kar sakte hain?
Haan. Manager plan mein aap multiple teams manage kar sakte hain. Har team ka alag team code hota hai (jaise TEAMFIRE, TEAMSIMRAN). Leads automatically sahi team ko route hoti hain apne source ke hisaab se.
