Manual aur automated lead distribution mein kya fark hai?
Manual distribution: Manager lead receive karta hai → decide karta hai kaun sahi rahega → WhatsApp pe forward karta hai → member dekhta hai → call karta hai. Total time: 30 min to 6 hours. Automated distribution: Lead form submit → system automatically assign → member ko push notification → member turant call karta hai. Total time: 10 seconds. Automated mein human error, delays, aur unfairness bilkul nahi.
Lead automation ke baad manager ko kya karna hota hai?
Lead automation ke baad manager sirf high-level tracking karta hai — koi lead routing nahi karta. Manager ka role hota hai: (1) Dashboard mein team performance dekhna, (2) New members add karna, (3) Quota renew karna jab khatam ho, (4) Strategy decisions lena. Roz ka lead routing, WhatsApp pe sharing, Excel mein tracking — yeh sab completely band ho jaata hai.
