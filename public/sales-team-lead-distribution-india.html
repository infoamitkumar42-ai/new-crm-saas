🚩 Manual Distribution Mein Time Waste

Manager roz 30-60 minute lead routing mein waste karta hai. Facebook leads download, Excel mein paste, WhatsApp pe share — yeh sab band hota hai LeadFlow se. Ek baar setup — hamesha automatic.

🚩 Excel Sheet Tracking Fail Hoti Hai

Excel mein leads track karna — koi update nahi karta, data stale rehta hai, real-time visibility nahi. LeadFlow ka dashboard live hai — koi bhi lead ka status ek click mein dekho.

🚩 No Real-Time Visibility

Sales manager ko nahi pata right now kaun si lead kiske paas hai, kaun follow up kar raha hai, kaun idle baith ke time waste kar raha hai. Real-time dashboard sab kuch live dikhaata hai.

🚩 Unequal Workload — Team Conflict

Kuch sales members zyada leads le jaate hain, kuch ko kuch nahi milta. Yeh unfairness team mein resentment aur conflict create karti hai. Round-robin se sabko exactly equal leads milti hain.

🚩 Manager Bottleneck Banta Hai

Jab bhi lead aaye, team manager ka wait karti hai distribution ke liye. Manager busy ho toh leads pile up ho jaati hain. Automation se manager bottleneck completely eliminate hota hai.

🚩 Night Mein Aayi Leads Miss Ho Jaati Hain

Facebook ad raat bhar chalta hai — leads aati hain, koi nahi hota. Subah manager ne dekha, tab tak lead 12 ghante purani. Night Backlog system subah 10 baje sab automatically assign karta hai.

FAQ — Sales Team Lead Distribution

Indian SMB sales managers ke common questions ke jawab.

Sales team mein leads automatically distribute kaise kare?
LeadFlow CRM mein Facebook Lead Ad ka webhook connect karo. Jab bhi lead aaye, system round-robin algorithm se available sales team member dhundhta hai aur automatically assign kar deta hai — 10 seconds ke andar. Member ko push notification milti hai. Manager ko manually kuch nahi karna padta.
Round robin lead distribution system kya hota hai?
Round-robin distribution ek fair queuing system hai jisme leads sequence mein har team member ko milti hain. Pehli lead Member A ko, doosri Member B ko, teesri Member C ko — aur phir A se wapas shuru. Isse sabko equal number of leads milti hain aur koi favoritism nahi hota. LeadFlow ka advanced round-robin daily limits aur online status bhi check karta hai.
Sales team CRM India mein kaunsa best hai?
Indian SMB sales teams ke liye LeadFlow CRM sabse practical hai kyunki yeh specifically Facebook Lead Ads ke saath kaam karta hai, mobile-first hai, aur quota-based pricing hai — monthly subscription nahi. Insurance, real estate, network marketing aur coaching sales teams use kar rahi hain.
Lead distribution software ka kya fayda hai?
Lead distribution software se: (1) Response time dramatically ghatta hai — leads seconds mein assign hoti hain. (2) Fair distribution hoti hai — koi member zyada ya kam leads nahi leta. (3) Manager ka time bachta hai — manual assignment band. (4) Night leads bhi handle hoti hain. (5) Real-time tracking milti hai — konsi lead kahan hai.
Sales team ke liye daily lead limit kaise set kare?
LeadFlow CRM mein har plan ka daily limit pre-set hota hai: Starter 5/day, Supervisor 10/day, Manager 15/day. Agar aap custom limit chahte hain toh admin panel se member ka plan upgrade ya downgrade karke adjust kar sakte hain. System automatically track karta hai ki din mein kitni leads assign hui hain aur limit aane par us member ko skip kar deta hai.
