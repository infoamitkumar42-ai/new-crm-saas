Network Marketing Teams Ki Yeh Problems Solve Hoti Hain

MLM business ke specific pain points ka targeted solution — ek generic CRM nahi.

🚩 Downline Mein Lead Confusion

Manager lead deta hai, 2 downline members same prospect ko call kar dete hain. Prospect frustrated, brand image kharab. Automatic single-assignment se yeh kabhi nahi hoga.

🚩 Lead Sharing Ka Chaos

WhatsApp group mein lead details share karna — screenshot, forward, phir track karo kisne call kiya. Network marketing mein yeh model scalable nahi hai. LeadFlow sab streamline karta hai.

🚩 Slow Follow-Up = Recruitment Miss

Network marketing mein prospect ka interest short-lived hota hai. Agar 2-3 ghante baad call karo toh woh kisi aur ke downline mein join ho chuka hoga. LeadFlow seconds mein assign karta hai.

🚩 No Visibility For Upline

Upline manager nahi jaanta koun se leads assign huin, kaun follow up kar raha hai, kaun active hai. Manager dashboard mein sab real-time — kaun online, kitni leads bachi, quota status.

🚩 Duplicate Assignments Baar Baar

Same lead multiple members ko mil jaati hai — ek distribution list mein aur doosri baar ek aur group mein. System-level deduplication se yeh problem hamesha ke liye khatam.

🚩 Raat Ke Leads Kaun Uthata Hai?

Facebook ad raat ko bhi chalta hai, leads aati hain. Network marketing teams raat ko offline hoti hain. Night Backlog system subah 10 baje automatically assign karta hai — koi lead barbaad nahi.

FAQ — Network Marketing CRM

MLM aur NM teams ke common questions ke jawab.

Network marketing ke liye best CRM India mein kaunsa hai?
LeadFlow CRM specifically Indian network marketing teams ke liye bana hai. Yeh Facebook leads ko automatically round-robin system se team members ko assign karta hai. Forever Living, Amway, Modicare, Vestige aur insurance teams use kar rahi hain. Generic CRMs NM workflow ko support nahi karte — LeadFlow karta hai.
MLM team mein leads kaise distribute kare automatically?
LeadFlow CRM ka Facebook webhook connect karo apne Lead Ad se. Jab koi prospect form fill karta hai, system automatically eligible team member dhundhta hai — is_active, is_online, daily limit remaining check karke. Phir lead assign hoti hai aur member ko push notification milti hai. Koi manual step nahi.
