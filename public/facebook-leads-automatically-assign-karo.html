Facebook leads automatically kaise assign kare?
Facebook leads automatically assign karne ke liye LeadFlow CRM use karo: (1) LeadFlow mein account banao, (2) Admin panel se webhook URL copy karo, (3) Facebook Lead Ad settings mein yeh URL paste karo (Meta Ads Manager → Lead Ads → Settings → CRM Integration), (4) Team members add karo. Iske baad jab bhi koi prospect lead form submit karega, LeadFlow automatically sahi team member ko 10 seconds mein assign kar dega.
Facebook Lead Ads ke baad kya hota hai lead ka?
Agar LeadFlow CRM connect hai: Prospect form submit karta hai → lead LeadFlow system mein aati hai → round-robin algorithm eligible team member choose karta hai → lead assign hoti hai → member ko push notification milti hai — sab 10 seconds mein. Agar LeadFlow connect nahi hai: Lead sirf Meta Ads Manager mein dikhti hai, aur manually download karke distribute karna padta hai.
Team ko leads automatically distribute karne ka system kya hai?
LeadFlow CRM ka round-robin system automatically leads distribute karta hai. Yeh system check karta hai: (1) Kaun online hai? (2) Kaun active hai? (3) Kiska daily quota remaining hai? Is sab ke basis pe best eligible member choose karke lead assign karta hai. Fair, fast, automatic — koi manager involvement nahi.
