Facebook Lead Ads Ke Baad Yeh Problems Aati Hain

Yeh sirf distribution tool nahi — yeh Facebook ads ke real pain points ka solution hai.

🚩 WhatsApp Screenshot Chaos

Facebook se lead aati hai, manager screenshot lete hain aur group mein bhejte hain. 3-4 log ek hi lead pe call karte hain, koi coordination nahi. LeadFlow se ek lead sirf ek member ko — koi confusion nahi.

🚩 Slow Response = Dead Lead

Facebook Lead Ads mein prospect ka interest sirf 30-60 minutes ka hota hai. Agar aap 3 ghante baad call karo, woh already doosre se baat kar chuka hoga. LeadFlow seconds mein assign karta hai.

🚩 Duplicate Calls Aur Prospect Ka Gussa

Same lead 2-3 log call karte hain, prospect frustrated ho jaata hai. Brand ki image kharab hoti hai. Automatic single-assignment se yeh kabhi nahi hoga.

🚩 Koi Tracking Nahi

Manager ko nahi pata kitni leads aayi, kitni assign hui, kaun follow up kar raha hai. LeadFlow ka real-time dashboard sab kuch dikhaata hai — ek jagah.

🚩 Raat Ki Leads Gum Ho Jaati Hain

Facebook ad raat ko bhi chalta hai, leads aati hain — koi online nahi hota, leads pile up karti hain aur subah koi nahi dekhta. Night Backlog system subah 10 baje automatically assign karta hai.

🚩 Unfair Lead Distribution

Senior members ya manager ke contacts zyada leads lete hain, juniors ko kuch nahi milta. Automatic round-robin se har member ko equal aur fair chance milta hai — koi favoritism nahi.
